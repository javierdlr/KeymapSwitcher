How to put KeymapSwitcher on your Workbench titlebar
(NOTE: this are the minimum options to create a screen titlebar dock)
 
Open AmiDock preferences and ADD(1) a new Docks ('Barra_Top').
Go to Dock→Contents tab and ADD..(2) KeymapSwitcher to Objects.
Make sure RESPECT SCREEN TITLEBAR(3) option is Off.
 
Drag&Move the new dock and place where you want KeymapSwitcher to be shown.
In Dock→Layout tab configure follow options as minimum:
ICON MINIMUM/MAXIMUM SIZE(1) to your WB screen titlebar height.
DOCK TYPE(5) 'Icons', ICON NAMES(2) 'None',
DOCK HAS DRAG BAR(3) Off and DOCKY IS BORDERLESS(4) On.
 
Go to Dock→Appearance tab and set ICON AREA(1) to 'Fully transparent'.
 
Leave Dock→Misc tab untouched.
 
SAVE preferences. Et voilà!
You can use 'Barra_Top' Docks to put there all new screenbar dockies available on os4depot.
